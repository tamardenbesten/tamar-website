I am a third-year doctoral candidate in Economics at Northwestern University. My research interests include empirical macroeconomics, monetary policy, and international economics.

Before Northwestern, I earned an M.Sc. in Economics from the Stockholm School of Economics and a B.Sc. in Psychology, Politics, Law, and Economics from the University of Amsterdam.

I have worked in the International Policy Analysis division at the European Central Bank and in the Monetary Policy department at De Nederlandsche Bank.

Research Interests

Macroeconomics, monetary and fiscal policy, international economics.
